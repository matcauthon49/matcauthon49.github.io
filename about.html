me

i grew up in bangalore, india and st. louis, missouri along with a brief stint in reading, england, but you're far more likely to see me in paris, france, or somewhere on the highway.

if you ever want to meet me at a conference, look for the guy in the eagles jersey. if you ever want to meet me outside of a conference, look around for the closest sailboat. i will probably have a cheeseburger in my hand, and if you're lucky even a diet coke. don't be afraid to say hi, as long as you follow it up with something about the fall of rome.
