film archival // 珊瑚岛上的死光 (death ray on coral island, 1980)

TL;DR — I have managed to track down a copy of this film, translated it and published full English subtitles for free online.

珊瑚岛上的死光 (pinyin: shanhu dao shang de shi guang) — known in English as “Death Ray on Coral Island” (dir. Hongmei Zhang, 1980) was the first science fiction film made in post-1949 China, as part of a brief golden age of Chinese science fiction under the early years of Deng Xiaoping. It was adapted from a short story of the same name authored by Tong Enzheng, the first-ever science fiction story published in China’s official literary magazine “Renmin wenxue”.

As far as I am aware, the only ever English translation of the film was as part of MoMA's Future Imperfect: The Uncanny in Science Fiction series presented in the summer of 2017, where it was screened with subtitles. MoMA speculates that this was an unofficial North American theatrical release of the film.

Anyway, I have managed to procure this film, and published a full translation with subtitles. As far as I am aware, these are the first publicly available English subtitles for the film.
