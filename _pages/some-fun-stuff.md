<h1 style="color: #cc0000;">some fun stuff</h1>

I often find that these academic webpages serve as cookie-cutter enterprises devoted to being easy paper-finding resources. Which isn't really a bad thing, per se, but they do come off as devoid of personality; the subject usually ends up seeming stoic or unapproachable. It's why I usually like adding a some irrelevant information about myself. It's quite fun.

<h2 style="color: #cc0000;">about me</h2>

I like travelling quite a bit and I've lived in a bunch of different places in my life. My favorite among these places is probably Bangalore, which happens to be my hometown, followed shortly by Paris. I also spent a fantastic year and a half in Chennai. Sometimes I write about these things. I also write short stories, satirical articles, and ocassionally poetry, although I've only recently started uploading some of my work. You can find it [here](https://unfortunateepigraphies.substack.com/). It's not a very good website, but I guess it suffices. Eventually I'd like to make something out of writing.

Naturally I read a lot of books. Hemingway is my favorite, though I'm fond of Raymond Carver, Joan Didion, Sylvia Plath and Bret Easton Ellis. The poetry I enjoy is also largely of the same era, particularly Plath, Leonard Cohen and Allen Ginsberg. All of these are somewhat countercultural in nature, starting from the beats (I keep a hardbound copy of <em>On the Road</em> with me at all times) to the hippies to the dirty realists to the brat pack. This also extends to cinema, which I'm a big fan of -- I'm especially fond of the American independent scene of the '90s and of Audrey Hepburn movies. I have a [letterboxd account](https://letterboxd.com/MatCauthon49/) which contains several reviews, most of which are irreverent, but I do think there are a few good ones in there. Here are some of my [favorite films](https://letterboxd.com/matcauthon49/list/all-time-favourite-movies/).

I play guitar! Not well, certainly, but I can slam power chords and pass it off as punk. I'm a big fan of noise rock, shoegaze and indie rock (and, sigh, Lana Del Rey). Most of the stuff I listen to is part of that indie-sphere comprising of bands like Sonic Youth, the Strokes, the Replacements, Yo La Tengo, the Cure, Simon and Garfunkel, Car Seat Headrest, Belle and Sebastian... you get the bill. I'm into pretty much anything art- related, honestly, whether it's music, literature, or literal visual art like a Monet or Van Gogh exhibition.

I enjoy sailing. One of my favorite experiences ever was sailing off the coast of [Le Havre](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Le_Havre) and [Etretat](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/%C3%89tretat). At some unspecified time in the future I hope to sail all the way across the English channel from Calais to the white cliffs of Dover.

I watch a lot of sports, though the only sport I regularly practice is running (and hiking? if you can call it one). I support Manchester United for no good reason, and I also have a soft spot for Borussia Dortmund. I also watch Formula 1, where I support McLaren. I enjoy arguing about football and F1. If you ever want to pick a fight, let me know. I'd be happy to oblige.

At some point I gave the mathematical olympiad. I did pretty okay.
