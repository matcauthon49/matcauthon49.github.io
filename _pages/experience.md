<h1 style="color: #cc0000;">Experience</h1>

I've worked on a variety of different projects as an intern or as a contributor. Some of my experiences are listed below.

#### 2023

+ **Research Intern, Institut de Recherche en Informatique Fondamentale.** (May 2023 - July 2023).
    Advised by Geoffroy Couteau. We worked on sublinear secure computation from homomorphic secret sharing.

+ **Cryptographic Engineering Intern, Panther Protocol.** (December 2022 - March 2023).
    Worked on implementing a pairing-based SNARK with a universally updateable CRS that can be instantiated with any pairing friendly curve. Details can be found [here](https://eprint.iacr.org/2023/1255).

#### 2022

+ **Research Intern, Tata Institute of Fundamental Research.** (May 2022 - December 2022).
    Advised by Akshayaram Srinivasan. We worked on PIR.

+ **Research Intern, Indian Institute of Science.** (May 2022 - December 2022).
    Advised by Chaya Ganesh. We worked on generalizing SNARKs used in Byzantine Agreement.

+ **JSOC Contributor, first xKDR, then Julia Computing.** (April 2022 - September 2022).
    Wrote the [TSFrames.js](https://github.com/xKDR/TSFrames.jl) package in Julia for time series analysis.
