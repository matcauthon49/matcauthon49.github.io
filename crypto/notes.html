crypto-notes

some notes i wrote on cryptography and/or computer science-related topics.

  • [onote] notes on o-notation. o-notation is a tool used to measure the asymptotic growth rate of functions. it's not complicated, but sometimes feels like it.
  • [pss] notes on packed secret sharing, a technique developed as an extension to shamir's secret sharing that allows the packing of multiple shared values into a single 'share' at the cost of lowering the security threshold.
  • [fft] an expose on the fast fourier transform and related fast polynomial multiplication algorithms.
  • [imp-rud] an expose on the famous 1989 result of impagliazzo and rudich that shows a black-box separation between key agreement and one-way functions.
