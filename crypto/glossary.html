crypto-glossary

an under-development cryptography glossary i am working on.

assumptions

  • [ddh] a survey of the decisional diffie-hellman problem.
  • [paillier] invented the paillier cryptosystem, a scheme with homomorphic properties that bases its foundations on the decisional composite residuosity problem.
  • [lattice-decade] peikert's survey on cryptography from lattices.

foundations

  • [crypto] the paper that started it all.
  • [merkle] introduces merkle's puzzles.
  • [zk] introduced interactive proofs and zero-knowledge.
  • [sem] formalized provable security.

random oracles

  • [rom] introduced the random oracle methodology as a technique for the security analysis of protocols.
  • [rom-revisited] constructs schemes which are secure in the rom but not when instantiated with any hash function.

(im)possibilities

  • [obf] shows the impossibility of black-box obfuscation.
  • [black-box] the first non-black-box technique to construct a zero-knowledge proof simulator.

encryption

  • [cramer-shoup] introduces the first cca-secure encryption scheme with a proof of security based only on the decisional diffie-hellman problem.
  • [qr2] introduces a provably kdm-secure encryption scheme from the qr assumption.

idealized models

  • [ggm] shoup's generic group model.
  • [agm] introduces the algebraic group model.
