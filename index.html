About Me

I am a third-year undergraduate student majoring in Mathematics and Computer Science at Chennai Mathematical Institute, India.

My research interests are in theoretical computer science with particular focus on cryptography and complexity theory. I am broadly interested in all aspects of cryptography, and have been fortunate to work on proof systems and secure multi-party computation. Recently I have also become interested in applications of cryptography to privacy-preserving technologies and blockchain.

Formerly I was a math olympiad participant, and maintain an interest in combinatorics and other discrete math.
